How to Play

1. Click on the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.
2. After three rolls, the game will total up the scores and display the winner.
3. If you want to start a new game, click the "Reset Game" button.

Rules for the Game

- Each player rolls a pair of dice 3 times.
- If any of the player's dice comes up as a 1, the score for that round is 0.
- If the player rolls a pair of the same numbers, the player's score is the total of the two dice times 2.
- If the player rolls any other combination of dice, the player's score is the total value of the two dice.
- The player with the highest score after 3 rounds wins the game!
